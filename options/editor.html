India on a Plate: Editor’s Pick of Must-Try Dishes!

Pani Puri
A crispy and tangy street food favorite, filled with spiced water, potatoes, and chickpeas.
Vada Pav
Mumbai’s beloved spicy potato fritter slider, served with chutneys and green chilies.
Hyderabadi Biryani
Fragrant, slow-cooked biryani with marinated meat, saffron, and aromatic spices.
Rogan Josh
A rich Kashmiri lamb curry with deep red gravy, infused with flavorful spices.
Chettinad Chicken Curry
A fiery Tamil Nadu-style chicken curry packed with black pepper and coconut.
Mysore Pak
A decadent South Indian sweet made from ghee, sugar, and gram flour, melting in every bite.
