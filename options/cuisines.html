Flavors of India: Most Popular Cuisines You Love

North Indian Cuisine: Butter Chicken
A creamy, rich tomato-based curry with tender tandoori chicken, best paired with naan.
South Indian Cuisine: Masala Dosa
A crispy, golden rice crepe filled with spiced potato masala, served with chutneys and sambar.
Mughlai Cuisine: Biryani
Aromatic, slow-cooked rice layered with marinated meat, saffron, and whole spices.
Gujarati Cuisine: Dhokla
Soft, spongy steamed gram flour cakes, lightly spiced and served with chutney.
Rajasthani Cuisine: Dal Baati Churma
Baked wheat dumplings served with spicy lentils and a sweet crumbled wheat dessert.
Bengali Cuisine: Rosogolla
Spongy, syrupy cottage cheese balls that melt in the mouth, a true Bengali delicacy.
